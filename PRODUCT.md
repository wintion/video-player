# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Rawya is for macOS users who play local audio and video files and want a clean native player that still keeps advanced playback, display, subtitle, playlist, and shortcut controls close at hand.

## Product Purpose

Rawya provides a simple, modern local media playback experience on macOS. Success means common playback tasks feel discoverable and low-friction, while power-user controls remain configurable without crowding the primary viewing experience.

## Brand Personality

Clean, modern, capable. The app should feel native to macOS, calm during playback, and confident when exposing advanced controls.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered player chrome, noisy onboarding, buried expert features with no visible path back to customization, and decorative UI that competes with the video.

## Design Principles

- Keep playback visually quiet and task-focused.
- Surface hidden capabilities at the point where users naturally look for them.
- Prefer native macOS affordances for menus, settings, shortcuts, and help.
- Let advanced users customize behavior without making defaults feel fragile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use standard AppKit controls and menu shortcuts so VoiceOver, keyboard navigation, localization, and reduced-motion expectations follow macOS conventions.
